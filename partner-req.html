Interested in partnering with Bloxco?

Thanks for your interest in partnering with Bloxco Supermarket. Before opening a Partnership Ticket, please make sure that your server meets our requirements mentioned below.

  1. Your Discord server must have 650+ members
  2. Your Roblox group must have 8k+ members
  3. Your group cannot sell SHR / high ranks
  4. Your group may not be a Military Group
  5. Your group must have a clean hisory
  6. Your group must be active
  7. Your main game must have 45k+ visits
  8. The community owner must be 13+ years of age
The requirements were last updated September 7th, 2023

Does your server meet these requirements? Amazing! Please open a partnership ticket in our Discord Server, and a member of Public Relations will help you further. We may decline your partnership request, even if you meet the requirements.
